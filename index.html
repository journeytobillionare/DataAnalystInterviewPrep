Interview Questions & Answers

Difference between COUNT and COUNTA?

COUNT counts only numeric values.
COUNTA counts all non-empty cells.

Difference between SUMIF and SUMIFS?

SUMIF works with one condition.
SUMIFS works with multiple conditions.

What is VLOOKUP?

VLOOKUP searches vertically in a table and returns matching values.

Why is XLOOKUP better than VLOOKUP?

XLOOKUP searches left and right and is more flexible.

What is QUERY Function?

QUERY retrieves filtered data similar to SQL.

What are Pivot Tables?

Pivot Tables summarize large datasets quickly.
